Comfort during Quarantine. Window art for neighbors.

When COVID-19 made us stay at home, Kiersten Short took it upon herself to provide comforting and inspirational messages to her neighbors by posting artwork in her kitchen window. More than 200 days later, these messages continue to support her local neighbors and social followers

Who knew? Working from home rocks!

Spending time to contemplate in the morning and attending online art classes with her family bring this artist joy.

Kiersten Short. The artist and book connoisseur.
